Process & Design

We take pride in handling all aspects of product development, from design to manufacturing and packaging. The very best craftsmanship and care goes into manufacturing the key organiser.
Each key organiser starts as a Italian leather hide. It’s carefully dyed, dried and then cut to shape. A vegetable tanned leather base is hand-bonded before stitching. The stainless steel parts are post-machined and checked for tolerances.

Final assembly includes adding the D-ring and packaging. The packaging is made from 100% recycled materials and is glued using biodegradable corn-starch.
We've worked closely with global suppliers to source the best materials and test our designs. Each key organiser made is checked more than once, ensuring you get the product we envisioned.
